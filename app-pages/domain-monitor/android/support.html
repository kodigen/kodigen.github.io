In the main screen, at the bottom right must be a button with a plus sign (+) inside. Click the button then write domain name i.e. "google.com" then click "Add".

There can be so many errors to prevent you to adding a domain.

If there is no specific error, please check possible errors below:

  • A trouble in your internet connection.
  • WHOIS server which is you requested is blocks you due too many requests.
  • Your ISP or VPN provider is blocking WHOIS requests.
  • Corruptions on WHOIS data. You can notify us about this.

Not all domain extension may supported. Some domain extensions like ".de" doesn't provide expiry date on their own WHOIS data.

For Android 8.0 and above versions are requires sticky notifications to keep background services work for security reasons. The notification system is dependent this background service.

Domain Monitor has a background service to monitor the changes of the domain, naturally. The domains you have added will scanned in order. The monitoring service will continue running in the background as long as you enabled. You can disable it from settings if you don't want to be notified.

Domain Monitor doesn't use any third party services to obtain the domain availability or expiry date information. Requests are send by directly from your device. But we're using Google Analytics, to analyze the user behaviour. You can reach our privacy policy and Google privacy policy.
